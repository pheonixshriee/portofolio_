About College

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering (SSGMCE) in Shegaon,Maharashtra, is a prominent engineering institution established in 1983. The college is known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, guided by the teachings of Sant Gajanan Maharaj. SSGMCE offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines and is affiliated with Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University. The college emphasizes quality education, research, and innovation, fostering a vibrant learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and a strong focus on ethics and values.
